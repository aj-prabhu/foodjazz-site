Food Jazz 06
The Method

Three things stand between you
and cooking intuitively.

01
Kitchen as Maker Space

Your kitchen is a tool, not a recipe holder. Stock it to improvise. Right pantry, right setup, zero friction between idea and plate.

02
Active Tasting

Most people eat. They don't taste. Building a flavor vocabulary is the skill that makes everything else click. Sour, fat, heat, texture — you learn to read them.

03
Recipe Reading

Recipes are ratios, not instructions. Once you see the pattern — fat to acid, protein to veg, heat to time — you stop needing them.

The outcome: You can walk into any kitchen, with any ingredients, and cook something good.

Every time.
