Coaching Agreement
Coaching Agreement
Between Akshay Prabhu ("Coach") operating as Food Jazz, and ("Client")
Program
Scope
One-month (30-day) in-home culinary coaching program consisting of: one kitchen audit session (~2 hours), four weekly in-person coaching sessions (~2 hours each), one grocery shopping trip, daily cooking prompts and feedback via text/voice memo, and a post-program dinner party attended by Coach as a guest.
Duration
The program begins on the agreed start date and runs for 30 consecutive days, plus a post-program dinner party scheduled at the Client's convenience. In-person session dates are scheduled collaboratively during the kitchen audit.
Location
All sessions take place at the Client's home kitchen. The grocery trip takes place at a mutually agreed store.
Payment
Fee
$500.00 USD (beta rate). Payment is due before Day 1 of the 30-day program via the provided Stripe payment link. The standard rate for this program is $1,000.
Refund Policy
If the Client is unsatisfied after the first week (7 days), a full refund will be provided, no questions asked. After Day 7, no refunds are available.
Food & Materials
Groceries & Equipment
The Client is responsible for purchasing all groceries, pantry items, and kitchen equipment per the personalized stocking plan. Coach may bring spices, ingredients, or small tools for demonstration purposes. Coach will provide a Chinese cleaver as a program gift.
Food Prepared During Sessions
Food prepared during in-person sessions is made collaboratively for instructional purposes. Both Coach and Client may consume food made during sessions. This is part of the learning experience.
Liability & Safety
Assumption of Risk
Cooking involves inherent risks including but not limited to: cuts, burns, hot oil splatter, and allergic reactions. The Client acknowledges these risks and agrees to exercise reasonable care during all cooking activities. Coach will prioritize safety instruction throughout the program.
Liability Waiver
The Client agrees to hold Coach harmless from any injuries, damages, or losses sustained during or as a result of the program, except in cases of gross negligence. Coach agrees to hold Client harmless from any injuries sustained by Coach while at the Client's home.
Dietary Restrictions
The Client is responsible for communicating all food allergies, intolerances, and dietary restrictions. Coach will accommodate all disclosed restrictions in the curriculum.
Photo, Video & Testimonial Release
The Client grants Coach permission to use photos, videos, text messages, and other content created during the program for marketing, social media, website, and promotional purposes. This includes but is not limited to: food photos, cooking progress documentation, text conversations, and testimonial quotes. Coach will not use content that reveals the Client's home address or other sensitive personal information. The Client may request specific content be excluded at any time.
Scheduling & Cancellation
In-person sessions may be rescheduled with 24 hours notice. If Coach needs to cancel, an equivalent makeup session will be scheduled. If the Client misses a session without notice, it counts as completed. Daily prompts and feedback continue regardless of session schedule.
