30 days to turn your kitchen from a source of stress into your favorite room in the house.

Food Jazz is a method for intuitive cooking — not following recipes, but learning to think like a cook. Like jazz musicians who improvise within a structure, you'll learn the fundamentals of flavor so you can riff freely with whatever's in your fridge. No decision fatigue. No stress. Just flow.

The Program
The 30-Day Experience
  • 01
    Weekly Kitchen Sessions
    Akshay comes to you. Your kitchen, your ingredients — real skills in your space.
  • 02
    Cook Something Every Day
    One real meal a day for 30 days. Not recipes — instincts. This is how the habit gets built.
  • 03
    Daily Challenges & Check-ins
    A new challenge sent to your phone each day. Feedback, accountability, momentum — all month.
  • 04
    Flavor Fundamentals
    The principles that make every meal improvable — not recipes, but a framework that lasts.
  • 05
    Kitchen Blueprint
    A full kitchen audit — layout, tools, pantry staples that make cooking effortless every day.
