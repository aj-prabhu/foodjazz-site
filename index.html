Sound familiar?

You open the fridge, stare at it, and close it again.

Maybe you rely on the same few dishes. Maybe you only cook when you have a recipe in hand. Maybe you've convinced yourself you're just "not a cook."

None of that is permanent. Cooking is a skill, not a talent. You just never learned to think like one.

Food Jazz isn't about techniques or recipes. It's about building a relationship with your kitchen that actually lasts.

The Approach

Intuition over recipes. Principles over rules.

Food Jazz is named after the way jazz musicians play — not reading notes, but improvising freely within a structure they understand deeply. That's what cooking should feel like.

Once you understand how flavors work and why ratios matter, you can walk into any kitchen with any ingredients and make something worth eating. No app, no recipe, no stress.

It's a complete reset of how you relate to food.

Not a class. A coaching relationship.

Akshay comes to your kitchen, works with your actual ingredients, and builds skills that stick.

30 days of real cooking.

One meal a day, every day. That's how habits form. Not once a week on the weekend.

The results outlast the program.

You don't need Akshay after 30 days. That's the point.
