Seven Tenets of Food Jazz

These aren't rules. They're the mental shifts that make everything else click.

You Are Having Fun

Food is meant to be shared. Cooking is a means of connection, therapy, self care, and love. Your mindset is the first step to liberation through easeful intuitive cooking.

Think Flavor, Not Procedure

Recipes are not prescriptive. It's more important to recreate the flavors than the precise procedures or ingredients.

Use What You Have

Resourcefulness and adaptability are key. This may involve using leftovers, incorporating seasonal produce, or finding creative uses for ingredients that might otherwise go to waste.

Stock for Success

Surround yourself with inspiration. Spices, sauces, and toppings to transform your raw ingredients — this provides the framework for you to improvise within.

Taste the World

Navigate the world with your tongue. The best way to learn is with your own senses — practice active tasting when you eat out, watch people cook, especially your grandma.

Your Opinion Matters

There is no right answer, just your answers. Cook what you and your people think tastes good.

Build a Flavor Pyramid

The perfect meal creates a balance of flavors throughout.
