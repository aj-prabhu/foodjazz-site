    How it works.

    One-on-one · In your kitchen · Bay Area

    • Weekly Kitchen Sessions

      I come to your kitchen and help you build intuition in the place where you actually cook.

    • Cook Something Every Day

      One real meal a day, not from a recipe. That's how the habit actually forms.

    • Daily Challenges & Check-ins

      Structured practice and accountability to lock in what you learn between sessions.

    • Final challenge: host a dinner party

      After 30 days, your friends and family come over and you cook them a real meal from scratch. A coming out party for the cook you've become.

    What you'll learn.

    Active Tasting

    Navigate the world with your tongue. Practice tasting when you eat out, watch people cook, and build your flavor vocabulary.

    Flavor Pyramid

    Learn to compose a meal that feels intentional. Not just a dish, but an experience.

    Your Kitchen → Creative Makerspace

    Stock your pantry with spices, sauces, and toppings that inspire. Set up a space you'll actually want to cook in.

    Think Flavor, Not Procedure

    Recipes aren't prescriptive. Learn to recreate flavors, not follow steps. Use what you have and cook what your people think tastes good.
