Does this sound familiar?

Most people cook the same five things on rotation — or don't cook at all.

Whether you panic when you're missing one ingredient, or just eat whatever's around and call it dinner — you're not a bad cook. You just never learned to think like one.

Food Jazz is not another recipe app. It's a complete reset of how you relate to your kitchen.

The Method

Intuitive cooking, not recipe following.

Food Jazz is a method for intuitive cooking — not following recipes, but learning to think like a cook. Like jazz musicians who improvise within a structure, you'll learn the fundamentals of flavor so you can riff freely with whatever's in your fridge.

No decision fatigue. No stress. Just flow.

The Program

The 30-Day Experience

Five elements. One month. A relationship with food that lasts the rest of your life.

01

Weekly Kitchen Sessions

Akshay comes to you. Your kitchen, your ingredients — we cook together and build real skills in your space.

02

Cook Something Every Day

One real meal a day for 30 days. Not recipes — instincts. This is how the habit gets built.

03

Daily Challenges & Check-ins

A new cooking challenge sent to your phone each day. Feedback, accountability, and momentum — all month.

04

Flavor Fundamentals

Learn how flavors actually work — not recipes to memorize, but principles that make every meal improvable.

05

Kitchen Blueprint

A full kitchen audit at the start — the layout, tools, and pantry staples that make cooking feel effortless every day, long after the 30 days are done.

After 30 Days

What you walk away with.

  • Cook at least one real meal a day — without a recipe
  • Make dinner faster than DoorDash can deliver it
  • Build a cooking habit that actually sticks this time
  • Confidently host a dinner party for people you care about
  • Feel calm and in flow in your kitchen instead of stressed
