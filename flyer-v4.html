What You'll Do
  • ✦ Weekly Kitchen Sessions. Akshay comes to your kitchen and helps you build your intuition in the place where you actually cook.

Final challenge: host a dinner party!
After 30 days, your friends and family come over and you cook them a real meal from scratch. It's a coming out party for the cook you've become.
What You'll Learn
Active Tasting
Navigate the world with your tongue. Practice tasting when you eat out, watch people cook, and build your flavor vocabulary.
Flavor Pyramid
Learn to compose a meal that feels intentional. Not just a dish, but an experience.
Your Kitchen → Creative Makerspace
Stock your pantry with spices, sauces, and toppings that inspire. Set up a space you'll actually want to cook in long after the program ends.
Think Flavor, Not Procedure
Recipes aren't prescriptive. Learn to recreate flavors, not follow steps. Use what you have and cook what your people think tastes good.
